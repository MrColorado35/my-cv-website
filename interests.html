Ballroom Dance

Even before my first contact with Aikido, when I was about 14 I was lucky to try Ballroom dance. It's not easy for such a young man to do something so 'girly' as a dance. Polish Scout Assossiation (ZHP)

Back when I was 13 I joined a Scout Team and it was one of the best decisions in my life. None of my other hobbies or interests (maybe except compulsive book reading) has such a strong impact on the man I became, as Scouting. People I met there, places I visited during campsites, all sorts of activities, beginning with campfires and sleeping in tent in the middle of forrest, through sailing, to some of extreme sports I had occasion to try- it all started here: ZHP
